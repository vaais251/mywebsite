DINNING

Our fresh and delious meals

Adventure Sarai Hotel Skardu offers its guests the opportunity to use the dining room, which can accommodate 30 people at the same time. We serve our guests tasty, nutritious, varied breakfasts and delicacies of Polish and Pakistani cuisine and unique dishes of local Balti cuisine. Here you can eat: Polish dumplings, pancakes and Silesian nudles, balti prapu, kisir, chicken mosku, chicken qorma, chicken karai. You will try zucchini soup, balti bale and many other dishes. You will drink coffee and eat a piece of cake or kheer

ACTIVITIES

Adventure Sarai Hotel Skardu is the place to start enjoying the many attractions available in the area. Karpocho Fort, Deosai National Park, Marsur Rock, Sarfaranga Desert, traces of Buddhist ancestors, unique monuments of Shigar and Khaplu are just a few of the attractions that are easily accessible to our guests. From here you can easily start trekking to the feet of the Karakoram giants or an expedition to one of the famous peaks dreamed of by the greatest mountain lovers. Our guests can also count on the possibility of organizing motorcycle or jeep trips.
